Ikati Esengxoweni

alternative, urban traditional, world-music band based in Nelson Mandela Bay

who is ikati esengxoweni?

Hailing from the Eastern Cape, Ikati Esengxoweni is a New Age Afrofuturistic duo with isiXhosa roots which proudly represents their identity.

Roughly translating to "The Cat in the Bag", Ikati Esengxoweni is composed of Lark Wantu and Anam Manyati, two of the most raw talents to come out of South Africa. Accompanied by electronic sounds, indigenous instruments, violin, bass, guitar and drums, the voices of Wantu and Manyati unleash a torrent of sounds and rhythms which emanate South African tradition.

Behold Lark CatGod Wantu

The roots of this musical prodigy can be traced back to the Rudulu Clan in Uitenhage on the Eastern Cape of South Africa. The daughter of a Choir Conductor, Lark grew up watching her Mother bring home awards & trophies. This inspired her to join her school's choir where she realized her passion for music.

She graduated from Nursing School, only to quit her job so she could live in her truth.

Witness Anam Manyati

Anam Manyati originates from the Nguni People in Nelson Mandela Bay, formerly known as Port Elizabeth. Experienced in Film Production, Sound Engineering & Theatre, Anam wields a variety of artistic skills and is known as the epitome of artistry & soul.

Her musical journey began at Riebeek College Girls Highschool choir, where she fell in love with music under the guidance of Mrs. Zietsman. Her children are her biggest motivation to stay true to her purpose.

who is ikati esengxoweni

We met in 2016 at an entertainment event, a few months later we performed together for the first time at Primavera Sound in Barcelona. The rest is History.

Ikati Esengxoweni translates to "Cat In The Bag", signifying two things. First we believe, like cats, we have nine lives. So with every adversity, we might face, we shall live to see another day. Second, with every performance, you may expect an element of surprise as we might pull a "cat out the bag".
